Free Travel in the West Midlands

If you are eligible for either our Older Person’s or Disabled Concessionary Travel Passes you can travel off-peak on the bus across the UK as well as on the train and tram within Network West Midlands area. Please visit either the Older Person’s or Disabled Person’s pages for more information on when and how you can apply or renew, eligibility and where and when you can travel.

Information on tickets for pre 09.30 and add-ons for out of area travel can be found using this Ticket Finder.
